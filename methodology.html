Clinical Data Transformation Engine (CDTE)

The CDTE is a deterministic processing layer that sits between the SCN intake point and the Payer's adjudication system. It performs three primary transformations:

01 Normalization

Maps disparate CBO intake forms to USCDI v3 and NYS 1115 statutory schemas.

02 Validation

Executes 18 NYCRR §521 MEAT (Monitor, Evaluate, Assess, Treat) documentation checks.

03 Attribution

Links clinical encounters to VBP risk-adjustment cohorts and SCN performance metrics.
